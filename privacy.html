RYVN Privacy Policy Last updated: April 26, 2026 RYVN is a personal strength-training tracker. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app touches and what it does not. The short version RYVN stores your data on your device. It does not run a server, does not transmit your workouts or account details anywhere, does not contain analytics or tracking SDKs, and does not share data with anyone. What we collect Account information you enter — display name, email address, and a password. These are stored only on your device and are used to identify your local profile within the app. Workout data you log — exercises, sets, weights, reps, rest times, notes, custom workouts, and challenge progress. Stored only on your device. App preferences — chosen weight units, theme, notification settings. Stored only on your device. How your data is stored All data lives in your device's local storage (browser localStorage for the web version, or its sandboxed equivalent inside the iOS / Android app). RYVN does not operate a backend service, so your data never reaches us, our employees, or any third party. What we do not do We do not transmit your account details, workouts, or device information off your device. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your data — there is nothing to sell because nothing leaves your device. We do not access your contacts, location, photos, microphone, camera, health data, or motion sensors. Your rights and controls Export your data — use the in-app Export feature to download a complete JSON snapshot of your account and history. Delete your data — use the in-app Reset feature, sign out, or uninstall the app. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data. You do not need to contact us to access, change, or delete your data — you control it directly inside the app. Children RYVN is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Because the app does not transmit data anywhere, no child's information would ever leave their device even if entered. Future changes If a future version of RYVN introduces optional cloud sync, social challenges, or any feature that transmits data, this policy will be updated before that feature is released, and the change will be noted in the app's release notes.
